Get Organized with Elicit

  1. Follow the instructions provided by your browser of choice → Export Bookmarks to HTML.
  2. Import the <yourFile>.html using Elicit's file uploader → Browse to location and select upload.
  3. Select bookmark(s) to be sorted.
  4. Select which topic the bookmark(s) should be organized under. If none exsist you may enter one by selecting → other. If no topic is selected the bookmark will be catagorized under "General".
  5. Select what sub topic the bookmark(s) may contain. If none exsist you may enter one by selecting → other. Selecting a sub topic is not neccessary.
  6. Select → Proceed to view your account's display.
  7. Click → inside a topic area to further explore available bookmarks under that topic.
  8. Click → bookmark name → to travel to that URL.
  9. Click → center of cirlce to move back up one layer.
  10. Click → Enjoy.
